I have been personally following Mara's language design work on the Rust project for some time now, and she does not disappoint: this book demystifies how low-level concurrency primitives work at the library, OS, and assembly level. In general there is tribal knowledge around how many of these topics work because of the lack of accessible resources. This book serves as a welcome surprise: it is difficult to find such a high-quality work in an ultra-specialized niche from an experienced expert. Mara generously hosts a [free transcript of this book on her website](https://mara.nl/atomics/), but I bought and read a soft cover as my form of gratuity (I also prefer physically reading a book).
